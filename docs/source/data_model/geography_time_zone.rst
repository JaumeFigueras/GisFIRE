Time zone
=========

The area on the ground over which one IANA time zone applies, kept so that a coordinate
can be turned into a zone *name*. See :doc:`../applications/time_zone_import_time_zones`
for where the polygons come from and why they cannot come from IANA itself, and
:doc:`wildfire` for the storage rule the names serve.
